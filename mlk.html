Martin Luther King Jr

Martin Luther King Jr. was best known for his infamous "I have a dream" speech and winning a Nobel Peace Prize. While others were advocating for freedom by 'any means necessary' including violence, Martin Luther King, Jr. used the power of words and acts of nonviolent resistance, such as protests, grassroots organizing, and civil disobedience to achieve seemingly-impossible goals.
