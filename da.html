Diane Abbott

Diane Abbot became the first black woman to become an MP in UK parliament. She has been the Member of Parliament for Hackney North and Stoke Newington since 1987 and still remains an MP today. She is a big achievement for black people in Britain and paved the way for other black people and ethnic minorities to persue politics.
